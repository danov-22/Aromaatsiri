About PT Aroma Atsiri Indonesia

Providing the best essential oils for our customers, guided by transparency and innovation.

Our Story

We Provide the Best Essential Oils for Our Customers

Aroma Atsiri Indonesia is a fast-growing company in the Indonesian essential oils industry. We believe that a company will only grow if it maintains transparency with its customers.

Our continuous investment in innovative technology keeps us at the forefront, allowing us to create new products and keep production costs to a minimum — savings we can pass on to our customers.

We have an in-house research team dedicated to discovering new and improved distillation techniques, as well as assisting our farming partners with good agricultural practices. With this, we continue to introduce new essential oils and natural derivatives.
